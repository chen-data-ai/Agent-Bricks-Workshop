# Digital Account Management Tools: User Guide

## Introduction
This guide covers the digital tools available to manage your telecom account, access billing information, make payments, and update your service preferences. Our digital platforms provide 24/7 access to your account from any device.

## Available Digital Account Management Options

### Mobile App
Our mobile application offers the most comprehensive account management experience:

1. **Download**: Search for "[Company] Mobile" in the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android)
2. **Registration**:
   - Install the app and tap "Register"
   - Enter your mobile number or account number
   - Complete the verification process (SMS code or email link)
   - Create a secure password (8+ characters with numbers and special characters)
   - Set up biometric authentication (fingerprint/face recognition) if desired

3. **Key Features**:
   - View and pay bills
   - Monitor data usage in real-time
   - Add/remove services
   - Report technical issues
   - Chat with customer support
   - Manage family lines

### Online Account Portal
For desktop access to your account:

1. **Registration**:
   - Visit our website and click "My Account"
   - Select "Register" and enter your account information
   - Verify your identity via email or SMS
   - Create your login credentials

2. **Key Features**:
   - All mobile app features
   - Download and print billing statements
   - Schedule automatic payments
   - Access detailed call/data history

## Security Considerations

- **Authentication**: Two-factor authentication is required for sensitive account changes
- **Session Timeout**: For your protection, you'll be automatically logged out after 15 minutes of inactivity
- **Device Management**: View and remove devices currently authorized to access your account
- **Notification Settings**: Enable alerts for login attempts, payment confirmations, and billing notices

## Account Changes & Documentation Requirements

| Change Type | Required Documentation | Processing Time | Available Channels |
|------------|------------------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Update contact info | None | Immediate | App, Online, Phone, In-store |
| Change payment method | Credit card/bank details | Immediate | App, Online, Phone, In-store |
| Add authorized user | ID verification of new user | 24-48 hours | Online, Phone, In-store |
| Transfer of ownership | ID verification, proof of relationship | 3-5 business days | Phone, In-store |
| Plan changes | None | Immediate to next billing cycle | App, Online, Phone, In-store |

## Troubleshooting

- **Forgotten Password**: Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login screen
- **Account Lockout**: After 5 failed login attempts, your account will be temporarily locked for 30 minutes
- **App Not Loading**: Ensure you have the latest version installed and adequate internet connection
- **Payment Issues**: Verify your payment information is current and try again or use an alternate payment method

## Alternative Account Management Methods

- **Phone Support**: Call 1-800-XXX-XXXX (available 8AM-8PM local time)
- **In-Store Assistance**: Visit any retail location with valid photo ID
- **Interactive Voice Response (IVR)**: Access basic account functions by calling our automated system

## Data Privacy

Your account information is encrypted and protected according to industry standards. We never share your personal information with third parties without your consent.

For additional assistance with digital account tools, contact our support team through any of the channels listed above.
