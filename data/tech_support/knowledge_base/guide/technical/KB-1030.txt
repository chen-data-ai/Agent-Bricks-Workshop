# Complete Guide to Wi-Fi Calling Setup

## What is Wi-Fi Calling?

Wi-Fi Calling allows you to make and receive calls and texts over a Wi-Fi network instead of using your cellular connection. This feature is especially useful in areas with weak cellular coverage but strong Wi-Fi signal.

## Prerequisites

Before setting up Wi-Fi Calling, ensure:

- Your device supports Wi-Fi Calling
- You have an active cellular plan that includes Wi-Fi Calling
- You're connected to a stable Wi-Fi network
- Your emergency address is registered with your carrier (for 911 calls)
- Your device software is up to date

## Setup Instructions by Device Type

### For iPhone (iOS)

1. **Access Settings**
   - Open the Settings app on your iPhone
   - _Expected result:_ Settings menu appears

2. **Navigate to Phone Settings**
   - Scroll down and tap "Phone"
   - _Expected result:_ Phone settings menu opens

3. **Enable Wi-Fi Calling**
   - Tap "Wi-Fi Calling"
   - Toggle "Wi-Fi Calling on This iPhone" to ON (green)
   - _Expected result:_ A popup will appear asking to confirm activation

4. **Confirm Emergency Address**
   - Enter or confirm your emergency address when prompted
   - Tap "Submit" or "Continue"
   - _Expected result:_ Address is saved and Wi-Fi Calling is activated

5. **Verify Activation**
   - Check your status bar for "Wi-Fi" or carrier name with "Wi-Fi" next to the signal bars
   - _Success indicator:_ "Wi-Fi" appears next to your carrier name in the status bar

### For Android Devices

1. **Access Settings**
   - Open the Settings app
   - _Expected result:_ Settings menu appears

2. **Navigate to Network Settings**
   - Tap "Connections," "Network & Internet," or "Mobile Network" (varies by device)
   - _Expected result:_ Network settings menu opens

3. **Access Calling Settings**
   - Tap "Mobile network," "Calls," or "Wi-Fi Calling" (varies by device)
   - _Expected result:_ Calling options appear

4. **Enable Wi-Fi Calling**
   - Toggle "Wi-Fi Calling" to ON
   - _Expected result:_ A popup appears for confirmation or address entry

5. **Confirm Emergency Address**
   - Enter your emergency address when prompted
   - Tap "Save" or "Continue"
   - _Expected result:_ Address is saved and Wi-Fi Calling is activated

6. **Verify Activation**
   - Look for a Wi-Fi Calling icon in your status bar
   - _Success indicator:_ Wi-Fi Calling icon appears in status bar or notification area

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Wi-Fi Calling Option Not Available

1. **Check Carrier Support**
   - Verify your plan includes Wi-Fi Calling through your carrier's app or website
   - _Expected result:_ Confirmation that your plan supports Wi-Fi Calling

2. **Update Device Software**
   - Go to Settings > System > Software Update (Android) or Settings > General > Software Update (iOS)
   - Install any available updates
   - _Expected result:_ Device software is current

3. **Reset Network Settings**
   - iOS: Settings > General > Reset > Reset Network Settings
   - Android: Settings > System > Reset options > Reset Wi-Fi, mobile & Bluetooth
   - _Expected result:_ Network settings return to default configuration
   - _Note:_ This will remove saved Wi-Fi networks and passwords

### Wi-Fi Calling Not Working

1. **Check Wi-Fi Connection**
   - Ensure you're connected to a stable Wi-Fi network
   - Try connecting to a different Wi-Fi network if available
   - _Expected result:_ Strong Wi-Fi connection established

2. **Toggle Airplane Mode**
   - Turn on Airplane Mode for 30 seconds, then turn it off
   - _Expected result:_ Network connections refresh

3. **Restart Your Device**
   - Power off your device completely, wait 30 seconds, then power it back on
   - _Expected result:_ All services restart properly

4. **Contact Your Carrier**
   - If issues persist, contact your carrier's technical support
