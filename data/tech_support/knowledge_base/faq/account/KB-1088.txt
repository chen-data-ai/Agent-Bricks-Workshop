# How do I change my account details?

## Overview
You can update various account details through our online portal, mobile app, or by contacting customer service. Different types of changes require different verification methods and have varying processing times.

## What account details can I change?

You can modify the following information:
- Contact information (phone number, email address)
- Billing address
- Payment method
- Account password/PIN
- Name on account (requires additional verification)
- Account ownership transfer (requires extensive verification)

## How to change your account details

### Online Account Management
1. Log in to your account at [my.provider.com](https://my.provider.com)
2. Navigate to "Account Settings" or "Profile"
3. Select the specific detail you wish to update
4. Follow the on-screen instructions to verify your identity
5. Submit your changes

### Mobile App
1. Open our mobile app (available on iOS and Android)
2. Tap on the "Account" or "Profile" section
3. Select "Edit" next to the information you want to change
4. Complete the verification process when prompted
5. Save your changes

### Customer Service
For assistance changing account details, contact our customer service at 1-800-XXX-XXXX. Please have your account number and identification ready.

## Verification Requirements

Different changes require different levels of verification:
- **Basic changes** (email, phone number): Account PIN or one-time verification code
- **Address changes**: Last 4 digits of payment method and account PIN
- **Payment method**: Verification of new payment details
- **Name changes**: Government-issued ID (can be uploaded securely online)
- **Account ownership**: Both current and new account holders must provide ID and complete a transfer form

## Processing Timeframes

- **Contact information**: Immediate
- **Billing address**: 1-2 business days
- **Payment method**: Immediate for credit/debit cards; 3-5 business days for bank accounts
- **Name changes**: 3-5 business days for processing
- **Account ownership**: 5-7 business days

## Impact on Billing and Services

- Changes to your billing address may affect applicable taxes and fees on your next bill
- Updating your payment method mid-billing cycle will not affect current charges
- Name changes will be reflected on your next bill
- Account ownership transfers may result in prorated charges on the final and first bills
- Some changes may trigger a temporary security hold on new purchases or plan changes (typically 24 hours)

## Important Notes

- Always ensure your contact information is current to receive important account notifications
- If you're moving to a new service area, you may need to review your service availability
- For business accounts, only authorized account administrators can make changes
- If you suspect unauthorized changes to your account, contact customer service immediately
