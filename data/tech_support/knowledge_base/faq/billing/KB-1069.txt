# What payment methods can I use to pay my bill?

We offer multiple convenient ways to pay your monthly bill to suit your preferences:

## Online Payment Options
- **Credit/Debit Cards**: We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover
- **Bank Account**: Set up direct payments from your checking or savings account
- **Digital Wallets**: Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal, and Venmo are available for quick payments

## Automatic Payment Options
- **AutoPay**: Enroll in AutoPay to have your bill amount automatically charged to your preferred payment method on your due date. You'll never miss a payment and can receive a $5 monthly discount on select plans.

## In-Person Payment Options
- **Retail Stores**: Pay at any of our store locations using cash, check, or card
- **Payment Kiosks**: Self-service payment kiosks are available 24/7 at select locations
- **Authorized Payment Centers**: Third-party retailers like Walmart, Western Union, or CheckFreePay (fees may apply)

## Other Payment Methods
- **By Phone**: Call our automated payment system at 1-800-XXX-XXXX (service fees may apply)
- **By Mail**: Send a check or money order to the address printed on your bill

## Payment Examples

If your monthly plan is $75.99 and you have device payments of $25.00, your total bill would be $100.99 plus applicable taxes and fees. With AutoPay enrollment on eligible plans, this could reduce to $95.99 plus taxes and fees.

## Managing Your Payments

You can manage all payment methods through:
- Our mobile app (available on iOS and Android)
- Your online account at www.ourcompany.com/myaccount
- Customer service at 1-800-XXX-XXXX

## Frequently Asked Questions

**When will my payment post to my account?**
- Online and phone payments typically post within 24 hours
- In-store payments post immediately
- Mailed payments may take 5-7 business days to process

**Is there a fee for making payments?**
- No fees for online payments through our website or app
- Some payment centers may charge a processing fee
- Express payment by phone with agent assistance incurs a $10 convenience fee

**What if I can't pay my full bill?**
We offer payment arrangements that can be set up through your online account or by calling customer service.

Need more help with your bill or payment options? Chat with us in the mobile app or call customer service at 1-800-XXX-XXXX.
