# FAQ: Why is my phone not receiving calls?

## Problem Overview
If your phone isn't receiving incoming calls, this could be due to network issues, device settings, account problems, or hardware malfunctions. This guide will help you identify and resolve the issue.

## Troubleshooting Steps

### 1. Check Network Connection
- Verify your phone shows adequate signal strength (at least 2-3 bars)
- Toggle Airplane Mode on for 30 seconds, then off again
- **iOS**: Go to Settings > Airplane Mode
- **Android**: Swipe down from the top of your screen and tap the Airplane Mode icon

### 2. Verify Call Forwarding is Disabled
Call forwarding may redirect your calls to another number.
- **iOS**: Go to Settings > Phone > Call Forwarding
- **Android**: Open the Phone app > Tap three dots (⋮) > Settings > Calls > Call Forwarding
- Ensure all forwarding options are turned off

### 3. Check for Call Blocking/Do Not Disturb
- **iOS**: 
  - Check Settings > Focus > Do Not Disturb
  - Go to Settings > Phone > Blocked Contacts
- **Android**:
  - Check Settings > Sound & Vibration > Do Not Disturb
  - Open Phone app > Three dots (⋮) > Settings > Blocked Numbers

### 4. Restart Your Device
- **iOS**: Press and hold side button + volume button until slider appears, slide to power off, then press and hold side button to restart
- **Android**: Press and hold power button, tap Restart

### 5. Check for Software Updates
- **iOS**: Settings > General > Software Update
- **Android**: Settings > System > System Update or Settings > Software Update

### 6. Reset Network Settings
**Warning**: This will remove saved Wi-Fi networks and Bluetooth pairings
- **iOS**: Settings > General > Reset > Reset Network Settings
- **Android**: Settings > System > Reset options > Reset Wi-Fi, mobile & Bluetooth

### 7. Check SIM Card
- Power off your device
- Remove SIM card using SIM ejector tool
- Check for damage or debris
- Reinsert properly and power on device

## Common Error Messages

- **"No Service"**: Your phone can't connect to the cellular network
- **"Call Failed"**: The network rejected the call attempt
- **"SIM Card Not Detected"**: Your device isn't recognizing the SIM card
- **"Cellular Network Not Available"**: Network coverage issues in your area

## Check Your Account Status

Use our mobile app or online account management portal to verify:
- Your account is active and in good standing
- There are no payment issues affecting service
- No recent changes to your plan that might impact calling features

**Mobile App**: 
- Log in to MyAccount app (iOS/Android)
- Go to Account > Service Status

**Online**:
- Visit [myaccount.ourcompany.com](http://myaccount.ourcompany.com)
- Log in and check Account Status
